📜 How to Play Leader's Jersey

Step 1: Join a Race

Go to 'Available races', pick an available Grand Tour or stage race and join before the first stage starts.

Step 2: Select Your Riders

For each stage, choose one rider you think will perform best.

Before stage 1, also select a backup rider — just in case your stage rider doesn't finish.

Be careful: the startlist might not be complete until one day before the start of the race, so check your selection on the morning of the start!

Step 3: Lock Your Selections

Selections close at the stage start time. After that, your choice is locked for that stage.

Step 4: Earn GC Time

  • 🎯 If your rider finishes the stage ➔ you get their time (minus bonus seconds if applicable).
  • 🚑 If your rider DNF's ➔ backup rider time is used.
  • 🐢 If both fail ➔ you get the time of the last finisher.

Step 5: Win!

The player with the lowest total GC time wins the race!
